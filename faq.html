Frequently Asked Questions

1. What makes us different from other servers?

What’s different about us is that we are not just a default framework; we use unique custom scripts. Also, everything on the server is made with great dedication, and you’ll see that when you come play with us.

2. Is the server whitelist-only?

Yes, the server is whitelist-only, and you must be at least 16 years old to play (although exceptions may apply).

3. How can I get whitelisted?

You need to join our Discord and from the webiste home page there is a "whitelist" button, After getting pending role in discord wait for a verbal test in the "Interview waiting".

4. How can I contact an admin if I have a problem?

If you have a question or need help, you can open a written ticket in the "ticket" channel or wait for one of us in the "Waiting Room" channel. Please provide as much information as possible about your issue to help us resolve it more easily.

5. What jobs can I take on in the server?

You have a wide variety of online jobs to choose from. There are restaurants, two garages, a hospital, a police department, a city hall, a car dealership, and a pawn shop. Each job is created with unique scripts that you may not have seen anywhere else!

6. Where can I buy a car?

The server has two car dealerships where you can buy a vehicle. In one, you can get a standard car (GTA 5 vanilla car), and in the other, a branded car. You can also buy a car with bitcoin (real money). To purchase a bitcoin car, browse the catalog of available cars, open a ticket, and let us know which car you want. We will then provide instructions on where to send the payment for the vehicle.

7. How can I buy a house or an apartment?

You can view available houses on the map, or if there are none, you can go to the city hall and submit an application for a private property of your choice.
